Precision Planting

As a representative for Precision Planting, we expanding our services to helping our customers with planting our seed - finding better spacing and depth to potentially improve their yields per acre - by a substantial increase.

Precision Planting's goal is to increase corn production by 12 to 16 bushels per acre. This is done through special technologies which improve seed spacing, depth control and enhancing seed environment.

We represent the following products:

  • 20/20 SeedSense - monitors true population data with real-time singulation and seed spacing - helps find errors when they happen
  • 20/20 AirForce - helps achieve consistent down force across all soil types and field conditions
  • 20/20 RowFlow - combines with SeedSense to provide automatic control to change plant population and to shut off individual rows or sections of the planter
  • John Deere and Kinze finger meters - Sell and repair these meters
